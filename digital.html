🔒 Digital Rights & Online Safety

In the digital age, knowing your rights and how to stay safe online is crucial.

📶 1. Right to Internet Access

Recognized as a fundamental right in some Indian states, Internet access empowers citizens with information, education, and services.

🔒 2. Data Privacy

As per the IT Act and the proposed Data Protection Bill, your personal data cannot be misused or shared without consent.

💬 3. Freedom of Expression

You have the right to express opinions online. However, this right is subject to reasonable restrictions (e.g., hate speech or incitement).

🛡 4. Cybercrime Protection

The Indian Cyber Crime Coordination Centre (I4C) helps fight online crimes like fraud, harassment, and identity theft.

👁 5. Digital Surveillance & Consent

You have the right to be informed if your data is being monitored. Consent is key before using any personal digital information.

📧 6. Reporting Abuse

You can report cyberbullying, fake profiles, or other digital threats at cybercrime.gov.in or call the helpline: 155260.
