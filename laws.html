⚖ Key Laws Every Citizen Should Know

Understanding your legal rights and duties is essential to be an empowered citizen.

🇮🇳 1. Constitution of India

The supreme law of the land, defining governance, rights, and democratic principles.

📘 2. Right to Information (RTI) Act, 2005

Empowers citizens to request government data, boosting transparency and accountability.

👥 3. Indian Penal Code (IPC), 1860

Outlines criminal offenses and penalties including theft, assault, fraud, and cybercrime.

🧒 4. Right to Education Act (RTE), 2009

Guarantees free and compulsory education to children aged 6–14 years across India.

🙅‍♀️ 5. Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005

Safeguards women from abuse—physical, emotional, sexual, or financial—within domestic relationships.

🌐 6. Information Technology (IT) Act, 2000

Regulates online conduct, digital privacy, e-commerce, and cybercrime enforcement.

📄 7. Consumer Protection Act, 2019

Protects consumers against unfair practices and ensures timely grievance redressal.
