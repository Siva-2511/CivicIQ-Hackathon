🌍 About CivIQ

CivIQ is a dynamic and user-friendly civic education platform designed to empower students, young citizens, and lifelong learners with essential knowledge about their rights, responsibilities, and the foundations of governance. From environmental duties to digital ethics, electoral processes to legal awareness, CivIQ brings clarity and engagement to topics that shape informed citizenship. Through concise explanations, myth-busting insights, interactive quizzes, and progress tracking features, CivIQ transforms civic learning into an accessible and impactful experience — one subject at a time.

Optimized for accessibility across devices, CivIQ offers a seamless experience with support for both light and dark modes. The platform is built with a responsive front-end using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and Chart.js, ensuring smooth performance and engaging visuals.

Whether you're preparing for a civics exam or just curious about how governance and rights work in your country, CivIQ helps you become an informed citizen — one topic at a time.
